A: Your existing comprehensive insurance is sufficient. We recommend informing your insurer about occasional rentals. We're working on additional trip protection for extra peace of mind.

**Q: How do you verify guests?**
A: Every guest must verify their Aadhaar, driving license, and phone number before booking. They also build ratings over time, visible to you before accepting.

**Q: Can I reject booking requests?**
A: Absolutely. You have full control. See the guest's profile, rating, and trip purpose before deciding. No automatic bookings.

**Q: What's the minimum booking?**
A: You set this. We recommend 4 hours minimum, but many hosts prefer 24-hour minimum.

**Q: How do I get paid?**
A: Weekly bank transfers every Wednesday for the previous week's completed trips. Track everything in the app.

**Q: What if the guest returns late?**
A: Late return fees (â‚¹200/hour) are charged to the guest and added to your payout.

**Q: Is this legal?**
