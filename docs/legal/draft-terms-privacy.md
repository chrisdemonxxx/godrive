7.1 Guests must:

8.2 **Host Payouts**: Processed weekly (every Wednesday) for completed Bookings from the prior week. Payouts are made to the bank account registered with us.

8.3 **Platform Fee**: GoDrive charges Hosts 20% of the Booking Fee as a service fee.

8.4 **Taxes**: All amounts are subject to GST at applicable rates (currently 18% on rental services).

9.3 If damages exceed the Security Deposit, the Guest is liable for the excess amount.
